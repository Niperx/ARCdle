ARCdle — daily ARC Raiders mini-games

It's a web game where you have to guess weapons based on their characteristics or sounds, maps based on a screenshot fragment, and more. Everyone has the same tasks each day, and they change daily for all players.

Daily challenge modes

  • Weapon — guess the weapon based on its characteristics.
  • Location — guess the map based on a screenshot fragment.
  • Blueprint — guess the blueprint based on components and letters.
  • Sound — guess the sound from ARC Raiders.
  • Skill — guess the skill based on the description.

Extra mini-games

  • Exodus Run — try to escape from Speranza and show how much you looted.
  • Favorite — choose your favorite among ARC enemies, dealers, and guns.
  • Build — generate a random loadout for your next raid.
